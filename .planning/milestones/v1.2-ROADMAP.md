# Milestone v1.2: shadcn/ui & Premium Dark Theme

**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2025-12-28
**Phases:** 11-13
**Total Plans:** 8

## Overview

Redesign the entire app with shadcn/ui components and a Proximab-inspired premium dark theme featuring purple/pink gradients, radial glows, glassmorphism, and scroll effects. Visual polish only — no new features.

**Reference:** https://proximab.framer.website/
- Purple/pink accent gradients on dark backgrounds (#06040d base)
- Glassmorphic cards with blur effects and subtle borders
- Radial glow effects for atmosphere
- Smooth scroll animations and sticky elements
- Typography: Poppins + Inter
