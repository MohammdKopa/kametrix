## v1.0 MVP (Shipped: 2025-12-27)

**Delivered:** Self-serve voice AI platform enabling small businesses to deploy AI receptionists with phone support, appointment booking, and credit-based billing in minutes.

**Phases completed:** 1-6 (21 plans total)

**Key accomplishments:**
- Email/password authentication with bcrypt, session cookies, and protected route middleware
- User dashboard with agent management, call history, and credit balance views
- Admin dashboard with full control over users, agents, phone numbers, and credits
- Vapi integration for voice agent creation, phone provisioning, and call handling
- Google OAuth with Calendar booking and Sheets auto-logging
- Stripe credit packs with purchase flow, usage tracking, and grace period handling
- Email notifications (welcome, low credits) via Nodemailer
- End-to-end testing verified, deployment documentation complete

**Stats:**
- 114 files created/modified
- 25,095 lines of TypeScript/TSX
- 6 phases, 21 plans
- 4 days from start to ship (Dec 24 → Dec 27, 2025)

**Git range:** `feat(01-01)` → `feat(06-03)`

**What's next:** v1.1 German Market & Polish - localization, UI redesign, landing page
